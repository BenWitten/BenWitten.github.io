About BdubD Enterprises

BdubD Enterprises is an event production agency that operates in the Greater Rochester area. BdubD Enterprises has thrown events ranging from large-scale nightlife events to bar crawls to concerts. It was founded by two students who recently graduated with MBAs in Marketing, Brand Management, and Entrepreneurship from the University of Rochester’s Simon School of Business.

We work with local businesses and different organizations to orchestrate weekly events targeting young adults. We hire and manage a team of contracted employees and have already attracted tens of thousands of attendees to different venues in Rochester nightlife. We proudly serve to benefit the greater Rochester community, promoting fun and community interaction.
